RATSASAN

Ratsasan is a Tamil psychological thriller film


Hero : Vishnu Vishal as Arun Kumar
Heroine : Amala Paul as Viji
Supporting role : Monica as Kayal and Saravanan as George
Director : Ram Kumar
Producer : G. Dilli Babu, R.Sridhar
Writter : Ram Kumar
Music Composer : Ghibran
Editor : San lokesh
Cinematography : P.V.Sankar
