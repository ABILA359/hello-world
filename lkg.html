LKG

LKG is a Tamil political satire film


Hero : RJ Balaji as Lalgudi Karupaiah Gandhi
Heroine : Priya Anand as Sarala Munusamy
Supporting role : Nanjil Sampath as Azhagu Meiyappan
Director : K.R.Prabhu
Producer : RJ Balaji
Writter : AR Murugadoss
Music Composer : Leon James
Editor : Anthony
Cinematography : Vidhu Ayyanna
